Strawberry

Strawberry ice cream is a flavor of ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar used to make ice cream

Butterscotch

Butterscotch is a type of confectionery whose primary ingredients are brown sugar and butter. Some recipes include corn syrup, cream, vanilla, and salt.

Pistachio

Pistachio ice cream or pistachio nut ice cream is an ice cream flavor made with pistachio nuts or flavoring. It is often distinctively green in color. Pistachio is also a flavor of sorbet and gelato.
